Tunde Balogun
About Speaker

Tunde Daniel Balogun is an astute entrepreneur, dogged real estate expert and a proficient business analyst. Charged with an overwhelming passion for changing and redefining the murky, disarrayed and outdated real estate system in Nigeria, he Co-founded RentSmallSmall, a Techstars backed real estate company that leverages on technology to provide solutions including property letting services which allows tenants to pay rent monthly whilst providing vetted quality tenants for Landlords. RentSmallSmall also launched Buy2Let, a property investment solution that allows Landlords to buy high yield investment properties and start earning guaranteed rent from day one of purchase.

Prior to founding RentSmallSmall, Tunde Balogun co-founded Unique Edge Management, a property management company based in Bangkok that managed a staggering portfolio of over 200 luxury properties in the U.S.A and Thailand before he moved back to Nigeria in 2015.

He holds a Bachelor’s degree in International Business Management as well as a Master’s Degree in Business Administration from Stamford University. He is a recipient of the 2012 edition of the Clinton Global Initiative held in Washington D.C. He is a Global Ambassador for JumpStart Africa, an empowerment program dedicated to bolstering young Africans for leadership roles and opportunities, as well as in business and entrepreneurship. He is also an executive director with Youths 4 Change, a movement that was created and borne out of the need to strategically implement changes in the socio-economic, and cultural maladies that ails our immediate environment and wider contexts within the country.

Abdul-Jabbar Oyekan
About Speaker

Abdul-Jabbar Oyekan is Founder of the Yahshud Charity platform. An Islamic crowdfunding platform that is focused on real estate investments and charity projects. He is also the CEO of Crystaldrive Construction Limited Lagos; a property development company focused on residential property development.

He is also the founder of the crystal shelter initiative; a non-profit initiative focused on providing subsidized and affordable housing to essential workers in the society.

With over 9 years in the property development industry, AbdulJabbar is an integrator of the disciplines of property marketing, construction technology and project management with a technical understanding of the considerations associated with management, design and development of property.

Ayo Akinmade
About Speaker

Ayo Akinmade is the Executive Vice Chairman, Regus in Nigeria. He has held this position since November 2016 when he merged his successful Regus franchise operations, with the globally acclaimed leaders in virtual office services. Before November 2016, he had operated his Regus franchise from 2007 to 2016 covering operations in Nigeria and Ghana, with locations in Lagos, Abuja, Port Harcourt and Accra.

Ayo has over three decades of investment experience in West African markets, managing business interests across E-commerce, Oil & Gas, IT and Financial Advisory Services. He serves on the board of several businesses including Mallforafrica, Frontier Capital, Savant Integrated Services Ltd, Doheney Services Ltd, Saro AgroSciences Ltd, Blueprint Business Technologies Ltd amongst others.

He trained with Price Waterhouse in the mid-eighties, worked in banking with International Merchant Bank in Nigeria in the late eighties, and for a decade, worked in the United Kingdom in Value Management & Audit roles with Milk Marketing Board and Milk Marque Ltd. He was also a Change Management Consultant in the UK, for over from 1995 to 2000, working with companies such as Ryder Plc and British Airways.

Ayo Akinmade holds a first degree in Economics from the University of Ife (1984), completed his ICAN professional examinations in May 1988 (PE II - Best overall student in Nigeria) and was awarded fellowship of the Institute of Chartered Accountants of Nigeria in 1993. His MBA program at the Kingston University, London in 1993 specialized in Change Management.

He is married with kids and is passionate about helping young entrepreneurs achieve their business objectives.

Executive Vice Chairman, Regus, Nigeria
